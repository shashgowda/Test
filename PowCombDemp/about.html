Company Description


Shenzhen Power Combine Intelligent Technology Co.,LTD. , located in Shenzhen, Guangdong China, is a collection of professional research , development and selling of DC micro-motor company. Our company own professional research and development equipment and advanced management concepts, and gather a large number of advanced technical and management personnel in this filed. We have a strong product development and production capacity. Our main products include metal brush motor, carton brush motor, gear box motor, brushless motor, and others such as steel pipe motors. Our motors are widely used in medical, household, beauty, health care, car model, aircraft model, electrical and electronic tools, office appliances, banking, communications equipment and other fields.



Power Combine’s philosophy is Quality first & Customer orientation & Win-win Companionship. After continuous efforts and innovation, we got the ISO/9001 quality management system certification and ISO/TS16949 automotive quality management system certification, which provide sufficient guarantee for the production of customer satisfaction, quality first-class products. Thanks to the majority of customers’ trust and support, Power Combine is growing healthily and fast. Our products have exported to Europe, America, southeast Asia other countries and regions, which win the Panasonic, Johnson & Johnson, Philips, ,OMRON, ReckittBenckiser ,Comfort and other international well-known enterprise’s consistent trust and praise.
